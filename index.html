ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Kinga. Until now I've been a air quality modeller at the University of Wrocław. I've been working with a meteorological model written in Fortran and C++. I have experience with bash scripting, R, vim and a little bit of python and NCL.

To be able to better understand atmospheric processes, I studied physics at Wrocław University of Technology and Science, where I also learned the basics of Fortran and objective programming in C++.

For the last few months, I have been learning HTML5/CSS3 and Javascript after hours. I always fully commit to every project I work on. I enjoy learning new technologies, and my latest tech crush is Vue :)
